// About Us

Modern Data Platform Pioneers - Built on Trust

KANTHERI Consulting (KC) helps enterprises turn data and AI into a sustained operating advantage. We pioneered lakehouse principles in Australia and New Zealand and are recognised for delivering comprehensive, enterprise-grade lakehouse platforms that scale with the business.

Building on this foundation, we design and engineer AI-native data platforms where intelligence is embedded directly into the data layer. These platforms are governed, secure, and built for day-to-day business operations. They replace fragmented analytics and experimental AI with decision-grade intelligence that supports automation, confidence, and long-term scale.

KC is trusted by CIOs, CDOs, and senior technology leaders to modernise complex data estates without disruption. Our approach is deliberately pragmatic. We align data and AI strategy to business priorities, design future-ready architectures, and execute with discipline using proven accelerators and iterative delivery. This results in predictable outcomes, faster time to value, and lower delivery risk.

We do not bolt on AI models. We engineer modern, lakehouse-based data platforms that make advanced analytics and AI reliable, repeatable, and embedded into everyday business operations. Data and AI do not just inform the business. They help run it.
